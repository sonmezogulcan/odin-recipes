Spaghetti

Description

This spaghetti recipe is a classic and delicious way to enjoy the true essence of pasta. The spaghetti noodles are cooked until perfectly tender. With its minimalistic approach, this recipe provides a versatile base that can be personalized with your preferred ingredients. Whether you desire a quick and satisfying weeknight meal or a blank canvas to explore your culinary creativity, this spaghetti recipe is an excellent choice that is both effortless to make and thoroughly enjoyable to savor.

Ingredients

  • 250g of spaghetti noodles
  • 1/2 tablespoon of salt
  • 4 liters of water

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add a 1/2 tablespoon of salt.
  2. Add 250 grams of spaghetti noodles to the boiling water.
  3. Cook the spaghetti until tender.
  4. Drain the cooked spaghetti in a colander.
  5. Transfer the spaghetti to a serving dish.
  6. Serve the spaghetti immediately.
